Fast Food | QSR

Fast food restaurants represent one of the largest segments of the food industry with over 200,000 restaurants and $120B in sales in the U.S. alone. Human Touch has a wealth of experience gathered through working closely with leaders in the QSR space which it deploys to assist it’s clients to improve and excel in customer service to drive sales and profitability.

The US fast food industry is growing faster than the restaurant industry as a whole due to comparative cost advantage and an increasing young population. Though consumption has declined during global crisis, economic recovery has seen business pushed back towards fast food as cost-conscious consumers are lured by cheap and innovative menu offerings.

Despite increasing sales, with consumer wallets squeezed due to all-time high food and fuel prices, food chain players are seeing margins fall. In the face of intense competition QSR players are deciding to absorb much of the increase in costs and pass on minimal hikes to consumers.
