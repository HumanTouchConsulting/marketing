RYG™ PERFORMANCE REPORT

 

You will receive your RYG™ Performance Report within 24-48 hours. The report is generated by Customer Service Specialists who view each video shop and rate the Team Member’s performance. The report details the performance of each store visit, and a summary report is compiled for each area, district, market or region, as well as the over-all company.

The report is sent to you by e-mail, as a PDF file, with in 24-48 hours after the video shops are completed. You can print out the report in part or in it’s entirety or choose to view it electronically.
