CUSTOMER SERVICE STANDARD

 

Creating the Customer Service Standard is the first step in implementing the Human Touch 4-Step Program. Human Touch helps clients define what specific behaviors drive their customer service, and then provides clients with a customized Customer Service Standard model. Tailored to meet each client’s specific customer service goals, the CSS, consists of proven behaviors that turn customer service into increased sales. Using the Customer Service Standard for training team members, who consistently implement these behaviors will bring customers back, and in-turn increase sales.

The CSS will contain different sections, depending on the specific client and the industry, below is an example of the 6 main sections a CSS may be divided into:

 
• Curb appeal of the location
• Greeting and welcoming the customer
• Assisting the customer
• Presentation to the customer
• Completing the sale
• Closing the sale
