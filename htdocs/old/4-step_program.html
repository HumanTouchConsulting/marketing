HUMAN TOUCH 4-STEP PROGRAM

I. Greeting the Customer

  1. Greeting the customer immediately as they come through the door.
  2. Make eye contact.
  3. Smile with a friendly smile.
  4. Greet with a friendly greeting using appropriate words.
  5. Pay full and undivided attention to the customer. Make the customer feel they are welcome and that their business is important to you and that you are happy to see them.

II. The Presentation:

  1. Pay full and undivided attention to the customer during the entire transaction.
  2. Qualify the customers’ needs to determine the appropriate product or service
  3. Suggest a specific and appropriate product or service.
  4. Presents the product or service, thoroughly and with knowledge.
  5. Ask the customer for their business.

III. Completing the Sale

  1. Pay full and undivided attention to the customer during the entire transaction.
  2. Verify the order by repeating it back to the customer.
  3. Offer add-ons.
  4. Give the customer your business card, repeat your name and tell the customer that if they have any questions or issues to please call you for assistance.
  5. Explain the process of paperwork and activation and the estimated time it will take.

IV. Concluding the Visit

  1. Look up.
  2. Make eye contact.
  3. Smile.
  4. Thank the customer for coming in.
  5. Invite the customer to come back.
