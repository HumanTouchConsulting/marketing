WEB INTERACTIVE TRAINING (WIT)

WIT - the Web Interactive Training program is a unique web based training program that provides near immediate coaching, targeting key behaviors where improvements are needed based on the results from the most recent Video Shop and the RYG™ Performance Rating.

Using the system is easy. Employees visit the Human Touch website and log into WIT using their employee ID# and their store#. Based on this information WIT looks up the performance results from the most recent Video Shop conducted at that store and checks where the performance was the lowest. WIT then customizes a training session focusing on the behaviors that were lacking for the employee to go through.

Each training session consists of the employee reviewing the customer service standard. If the employee has any questions about the standard they can click on a question mark next to the behavior for a detailed explanation accompanied by a video showing the correct behavior.

 

Each training session is approximately 10 minutes long. They begin with the First of three video clips demonstrating excellent examples of the expected behaviors and rate the performance using the "red," "yellow," or "green" scale. Should the answers not be correct the first time the employee has two more attempts to review the customer service model, consult with the detailed explanations and watch the video again to get it right. Once they have the correct answer they move on to the next video.

WIT generates a report of the results for management to see level of participation and results at the store level, area or distric
t, region or market and company overall. This allows managers to see how well each employee performed and identify opportunities to further coach employees on-on-one.

The report can be used as a basis for an incentive system to reward employees, stores, areas, districts, markets, and regions for participation and achievement.

WIT also offers a coaching assistance for managers offering suggestions and ideas of how to coach and motivate employees to improve their behaviors. This is customized to each store targeting key behaviors where improvements are needed based on the results from the most recent Video Shop and the RYG™ Performance Rating.
