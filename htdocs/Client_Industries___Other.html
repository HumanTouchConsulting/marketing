Jewelry

The jewelry industry is mature and despite volatility, has a long term stable future. It comprises five main segments: bridal jewelry, fashion jewelry, watches, precious stones and precious metals. In the US, jewelry retain is highly fragmented: Of about 28,000 specialty stores with combined annual revenue of about $30 billion, the top 50 jewelry chains generate less than half of revenue.

We assist our clients with a range of business issues that relate to the highly competitive nature of this industry, including competitor analysis, sales performance evaluation and training.
