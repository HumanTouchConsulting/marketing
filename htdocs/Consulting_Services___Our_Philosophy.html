Our Philosophy

We believe that the best way to help our clients measure, manage and make dramatic improvements to sales performance is to start with a crystal clear picture of operational reality as experienced by their customers.


The heart of every business is built around activities that create valuable products and services. However it is the customer interaction that determines whether or not this creation of value is converted into sales.

Over three decades Human Touch has built a wealth of expertise and experience that we bring to bear with our clients to assist them in optimizing the customer interaction. We bring a new level of insight into customer service and operational performance reality, enabling objective measurement, and therefore effective management.

HOW WE ADD VALUE

We base the value that we offer clients around measuring business reality.

Human Touch’s expert consultant pose as customers while wearing concealed video cameras to capture the customer experience. We then analyzes employee activities, to provide a near immediate and unprecedented insight into the company’s performance.

From known successful behaviors we structure and implement best-in-class customer service, incentive and performance models through simple, teachable and measurable changes.
