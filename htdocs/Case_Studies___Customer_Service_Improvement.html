Customer Service Improvement

Service quality issues


HTC was engaged to evaluate the quality of customer service, sales skills and increase customer service awareness among employees for a Canadian provisional bank with representation in over 240 communities.  HTC designed the evaluation criteria, customized a “Best of” competition between the branches and conducted the evaluation at all bank branches.


On-going customer service and sales improvement


For the past 10 years HTC has conducted a customer service and sales improvement programs for one of the largest fast food chains in the US.  HTC created a behavior model consisting of the specific behaviors designed to increase add on sales and customer satisfaction. HTC surveyed locations across the US on a monthly basis to monitor the progress and give operators a clear picture of where to focus their improvement efforts.
