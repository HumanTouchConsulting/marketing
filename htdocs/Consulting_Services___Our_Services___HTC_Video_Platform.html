HTC Video Platform

Human Touch consultants visit your locations and secretly video record their entire visit, while playing the role of a regular customer. The concealed video camera allows you to see, first hand, through the eye of the video camera, what your customers’ experience when visiting your business.

It’s as if you were looking at your own customer service through the eyes of your customers.


The video camera accurately captures every detail of the visit, details often missed or distorted by subjective observers relying on memory, questionable observation skills, and sometime insufficient experience to identify the correct behaviors.

Our industry-leading video workflow enables us to provide reporting of real life customer service interactions within 24 hours.
