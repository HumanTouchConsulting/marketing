Legal & Privacy


General

Human Touch, LLC. (“Human Touch”) respects the privacy of every individual who visits our website. This Privacy Statement outlines the information Human Touch will collect and how we will use that information.

Personally Identifiable Information

When you visit Human Touch's website, we will not collect any personally identifiable information about you (such as your name, address, telephone number, social security number, or e-mail address) unless you provide it to us voluntarily. Our website invites visitors to submit names and e-mail addresses in connection with providing specific notifications or to provide information. If you opt not to provide us with personally identifiable information no material consequences will result, although you may be unable to receive notification of fact sheet or press release availability, or receive product or company information.

Use of Personally Identifiable Information

Human Touch may store and process your personally identifiable information to better understand your needs and how we may improve our products and services, and we may use your personally identifiable information to contact you (for example, we may respond to your comments or requests for information, or we may contact you if needed in the course of processing or shipping your requests). Human Touch will not give or sell your personally identifiable information to any outside organization for the organization's own use without your consent. Individually identifiable information submitted in connection with employment inquiries will be used only in the application process.

The copy and claims contained in this website are directed at residents of the United States and are valid only in the United States. At times, if you direct comments to us or if you request information from us, we may ask you to verify that you live in the United States so that we can be assured that the response or information we provide is appropriate for your country of residence.

Security

Human Touch is committed to keeping secure the data you provide us and will take reasonable precautions to protect your personally identifiable information from loss, misuse or alteration. Agents or contractors of Human Touch who have access to your personally identifiable information in connection with providing services for Human Touch are required to keep the information confidential and are not permitted to use this information for any other purpose than to carry out the services they are performing for Human Touch.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information Collected Automatically

In some cases, we may collect information about you that is not personally identifiable. Examples of this type of information include the type of internet browser or computer operating system you are using and the domain name of the website from which you linked to our site.

Contacting Us Regarding Your Personally Identifiable Information

If you have submitted personally identifiable information through the Human Touch website, or if someone else has submitted your personally identifiable information through the Human Touch website, and you would like to have that information deleted from our databases, please contact us via e-mail at info@humantouch.com. You may also contact us through the above method to request the personally identifiable information that Human Touch has collected about you through its websites or interactive advertisements. Human Touch will use reasonable efforts to supply you with this information and correct any factual inaccuracies in this information.

Because information submitted in connection with employment inquiries is used only in the application process and not for marketing purposes, the procedures outlined in this Privacy Statement are not available with respect to such information.
